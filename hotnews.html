Mohamed Salah Clash

Mohamed Salah Timeline

Early October 2025

Salah is occasionally benched as Liverpool tries different tactical setups. Fans notice he’s not always starting.

Late November 2025

Salah is left out of the starting XI for multiple Premier League matches, including games against West Ham and Leeds.

Dec 6, 2025

Unused substitute in Liverpool’s 3-3 draw with Leeds. Post-match, Salah says he feels the club has “thrown me under the bus” and the relationship with manager Arne Slot is broken.

Dec 8, 2025

Left out of the squad for Liverpool’s Champions League trip to Inter Milan amid the fallout from his comments.

Dec 10, 2025

Club holds internal meeting with Salah and Arne Slot to address the conflict. Reports suggest tensions still high.

Dec 12, 2025

Manager hints Salah may return to starting XI soon but stresses team dynamics and discipline are priorities.

Dec 14, 2025

Salah starts against Southampton, signaling reconciliation with the manager. Fans respond positively to his return.

Turkey Football Betting Scandal

Turkey Timeline

27 October 2025

TFF audit reveals 371 referees had betting accounts, 152 were actively betting, violating FIFA and TFF rules.

31 October 2025

Istanbul Chief Public Prosecutor’s Office launches criminal investigation into referees’ betting activities.

10–11 November 2025

1,024 players suspended from professional football for alleged betting violations; lower division matches postponed.

11 November 2025

Eight arrests, including a top club chairman and referees; TFF calls it a “moral crisis” in Turkish football.

13 November 2025

102 professional players banned by TFF’s disciplinary board across Süper Lig and second division.

18 November 2025

281 players sanctioned; bans range from short-term to long-term exclusions from football.

26 November 2025

More than 1,100 suspensions across referees and players; lists published for transparency.

05 December 2025

Additional arrests, 46 individuals detained including club presidents, players, and referees.
